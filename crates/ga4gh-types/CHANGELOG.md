## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-12

### Added

- Shared Rust types for GA4GH Passports, Visas, DUO codes, and Service Info.
- Serde serialization with GA4GH JSON field naming and round-trip unit tests.
- `VisaType`, `VisaAuthority`, `VisaConditions`, and related passport/visa claim structs.
